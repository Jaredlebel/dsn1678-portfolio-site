Devil's IPA

Creative Direction · Branding · Design

Devil's IPA is an idea brewed from my own brain. I'm a big fan of a beer with taste. So naturally when it came to playing with some passion project ideas, Devil's IPA was born. I began with the logo and evolved the brand to consist of a set of icons which could be used across the collateral and cany campaigns this fictional craft brewery creates. This beer is not for the faint of heart and is best served while listening to At The Gates.
