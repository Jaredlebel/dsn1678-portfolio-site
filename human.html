Human 2.0

Brand development · Logo Design

Human 2.0 approached me and my partner Daniela Chavez to revamp their identity. They had a vision in mind and we came together to create a really great logo for them. The idea was born from the Greek God Prometheus. We wanted to depict the human shape, but to the next level.

We created a very thorough brand guideline so that they could use it throughout their brand. We didn't have the opportunity to work on the website for them but they've carried our brand through, so check it out!
