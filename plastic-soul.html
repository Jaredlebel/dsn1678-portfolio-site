Plastic Soul

Brand Development · Logo Design · Web Design

Alex, who is a friend and colleague, was a pleasure to work with in creating an edgy, yet corporate identity and website. His focus is on video production and editing, but he also creates his own short films, which are great! Because of his love for classic film and slick oriental typography, we decided to combine hand-done brush lettering with a sans-serif typeface to communicate his creativity as well as approachability.

We also had the opportunity to work on his website. The site is corporate and slick but still speaks levels about his creative eye. This is an asset he brings to the table and we wanted to make sure it was at the forefront. Take a look at his work here.
