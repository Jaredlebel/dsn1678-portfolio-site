Giving Tradition New Meaning

I am a graphic designer from Ottawa Ontario, Canada. I have a lot of fun making cool things and look forward to waking up every day with a challenge. I strive to take the traditional ideals of graphic design and merge them with the fast paced world we live in and opportunities that exist today. Most of all, I make compelling and beautiful designs that communicate the heart of every challenge.
