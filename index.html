Giving tradition New Meaning

I am a graphic designer from Ottawa Ontario, Canada.I have a lot of fun making cool shit and look forward to waking up every day with a challenge. I strive to take the traditional ideals of graphic design and merge them with the fast paced world we live in and opportunities that exist today. Most of all, I make compelling and beautiful designs that communicate the heart of every challenge.

I love meeting people. If you have an interesting idea, project or design in mind or just want to chat horror movies over a craft beer, don't hesitate to reach out to me and I promise not to blab on about all of my passions.
