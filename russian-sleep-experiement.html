The Russian Sleep Experiment

Creative Direction · Layout · Typography

The Russian Sleep Experiment is a fictional story originating from Creepy Pasta. Given my love for all things creepy, bloody and obscure, I gave myself the challenge of creating a visual experience with which to read the story. The goal was to create a beautiful layout that felt like the story should make you feel; uncomfortable. Laced with unsettling imagery and typography set as though it were a military document, I set the tone for the story and prepare the reader for a bizarre imaginative experience.
