Literra canina

Glyph Design

Literra canina is a glyph I made for type class. I drew inspiration from the Canadian National Railway logo and western slab serifs of the past. I wanted to create a geometric and modern approach to a classic Slab Serif style. We had the opportunity to work on three glyphs and posters to showcase each glyph. My plan is to push forward and create the whole typeface to put on the interwebz for free. I really enjoy designing glyphs and lettering and this was a really fun assignment. By the very end we were able to each print a 3D version of our glyph. I'm looking forward to seeing the complete typeface in action!
