Westboro Workers Cooperative

Creative Direction · Branding · Logo Design

Westboro Workers Cooperative was an idea for an initiative to promote the growth of local businesses in Westboro here in Ottawa. The idea was to have a membership card for employees of Westboro establishments. The money raised would then be donated to CHEO all the while supporting a community with a lot of culture.

The logo is hand drawn to represent the hard work that all the different types of local business put in to build the community. There are a number of icons within the logo which represent a number of the differnet types of businesses you would see in Westboro. Lastly, the logo is fun colourful to represent the nature and feeling of the initiative

This was a collaboration between me and my partner Daniela Chavez Ackermann who helped bring the idea to life and designed and developed the landing page for the site.
